Privacy Policy for VoxSherpa

Effective Date: March 28, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to VoxSherpa. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our Android application. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it.

2. Data Collection and Use

VoxSherpa is designed to respect user privacy. We do not collect, store, or share any personal user data. You are not required to create an account, provide an email address, or share any personal information to use the core features of this app.

3. Internet and Network Access

The app requires an internet connection solely for the following purpose:

  • Fetching AI Models: The app connects to our secure database (Google Firebase) to fetch the latest list of available AI voice models and download the necessary model files directly to your device.

Once a model is downloaded, the text-to-speech (TTS) generation happens 100% offline on your device.

4. Local Storage

VoxSherpa requests storage permissions to save the downloaded AI models and the audio files (WAV, AAC) that you generate. These files remain locally on your device and are never uploaded to any external server by us.

5. Third-Party Services

The app utilizes third-party services that may collect information used to identify you, purely for functional and security purposes (such as crash reporting or secure downloading).

  • Google Play Services
  • Google Firebase (used strictly for hosting and delivering AI model files)

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time as we add new features to VoxSherpa in future versions. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
